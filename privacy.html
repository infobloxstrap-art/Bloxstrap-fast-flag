Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 2, 2025

1. Summary

We collect minimal data (contact info you provide and analytics) to operate the site, respond to messages, and improve user experience. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how you can exercise your rights.

2. Data controller

BloxStrapFF — contact: support@bloxstrapff.netlify.app

3. What we collect

  • Contact info — name, email, message content you submit through contact forms.
  • Analytics — aggregated, anonymized metrics from third-party analytics (pageviews, device type).
  • Cookies — session and functional cookies for site features and analytics.
  • Logs — server logs for security and debugging (IP addresses retained for a limited time).

4. How we use data

To respond to inquiries, improve the site, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell personal data.

5. Third-party services

We may use third-party providers (analytics, hosting, email services). These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by their privacy policies. Examples: Netlify (hosting), Google Analytics (if used), email providers. Check provider policies for details.

6. Cookies & similar technologies

We use cookies for essential site function and analytics. You can control cookies through your browser; blocking cookies may affect site functionality.

7. Data retention

Contact messages and related metadata are retained as needed to handle requests and for legal compliance (generally up to 2 years unless otherwise required).

8. Security

We apply reasonable security measures (HTTPS, access controls). However, no transmission is 100% secure — if you have concerns, contact us for safer exchange options.

9. Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or portability of your personal data. To exercise rights, email support@bloxstrapff.netlify.app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

10. Children

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will post the effective date at the top and reasonable notice of material changes.

12. Contact

For privacy inquiries: support@bloxstrapff.netlify.app
