Frequently Asked Questions

What are Fast Flags?

Fast Flags are configuration settings that allow you to modify various aspects of Roblox's behavior and appearance. They can improve performance, change graphics settings, and enable experimental features.

Is using Fast Flags safe?

Yes, Fast Flags are generally safe to use as they only modify client-side settings. However, some flags may cause instability or unexpected behavior in certain games. Always backup your settings before making changes.

Can I get banned for using Fast Flags?

No, using Fast Flags with Bloxstrap is not against Roblox's Terms of Service as they only modify client-side settings and do not provide unfair advantages in gameplay.

How do I reset my Fast Flags?

To reset your Fast Flags, open Bloxstrap, go to Fast Flags > Fast Flags Editor, and click "Reset to Defaults". This will restore all settings to their original values.
